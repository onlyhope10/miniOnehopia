Who We Are?


MANBASE Solutions is an Innovative Human Resource Information System provider that caters to micro small and medium companies in the Philippines. Our current team that built our latest product software called HRIS-64, that hopes to support and empower our belief in Championing a growth mindset through our innovative and holistic cloud-based people platform, we will continue to empower Philippine businesses by making them ready for the future of work. Guided by our north star, which is to impact the lives of every Filipino by improving business in the Philippines, our goal is to ignite a transformational and digitized change in the HR industry as now, more than ever, we see the charge towards the new future of work! Our comprehensive product suite allows for seamless HR processing of the employee lifecycle and leveraging of enterprise data through invaluable analytics that aid in decision-making.

Our Services

Core HRIS

Providing systems that focuses on managing the core HR functions such as employee data management, payroll and benefits administration, time and attendance tracking, and compliance with labor laws and regulations.

Talent Management

Providing systems that are designed to manage and automate various talent-related processes such as recruitment, onboarding, performance management, succession planning, and employee development. These systems help organizations attract, retain, and develop top talent.

Analytics

Giving systems that provide advanced reporting and analytics capabilities, allowing organizations to generate insights and trends from HR data. They may include dashboards, data visualization tools, and predictive analytics to support data-driven decision-making.
